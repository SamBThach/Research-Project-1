Consent Form

The following is a consent form for a web-based matching game.

The web-based matching game consists of six images that will be matched to 6 phrases.

Privacy is important. The answers that participants submit will be collected and stored in a database.

Data stored will be held anonymously and will not hold any personal data that can be traced back to participants.

The stored data will only be accessible by verified researchers and will not be accessible by any person, organization, or company

Data will be used in the following ways:

  • Analyzed and published in a research paper

By checking and hitting submit, you agree to partake in the web-based matching game and for researchers to store and use your data in the above ways.
